About Me

After managing customer facing teams for the last ten years I was made redundant. A chance to work on a project to develop and launch an app during the last year I was with the business reignited my passion for tech and programming.

A keen musician, I have always played in bands, I spent the late nineties building band websites with Microsoft Frontpage and then the noughties customising myspace pages!
When I'm not learning to code I still play guitar and mandolin, binge box sets and horror movies, love to read, and explore the countryside of West Yorkshire. Visit my blog for more...
